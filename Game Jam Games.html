Game Jam Games

This section contains the information about all the games jam games that I was a part of.

Chaotic Divination

Chaotic Divination emerged from a 2-day game jam hosted by GMTK called Global Game Jam. It's a Co-op survival game where 2 gods which represent light and darkness are being attacked by their followers and each player represents a god. The goal is to survive as long as possible.

Out Of Control

Out of Control is an endless runner where robot "Dwain" is trying to escape from an exploding ship was made during a day long game jam hosted by the conestoga game dev club.


View On Itch

Time Capsule

A week-long game jam with a theme of rewind paved its way for Time capsule to be born. Time Capsule is a 2D puzzle game where the player has the ability to rewind time which aids them to escape from the government Facility.

Sumimasen Castle

Sumimasen Castle is a 2D adventure that has death as its core element. Upon death, the player switches between the overworld and the underworld, and he solves make his way through the castle to escape.
